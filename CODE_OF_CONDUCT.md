# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to a welcoming, harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our standards

Examples of positive behavior:
- Being respectful and constructive
- Giving actionable feedback
- Assuming good intent

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks or trolling
- Publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards. Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent bans.

## Reporting

Open a private security or moderation contact channel with maintainers for any urgent issues.
